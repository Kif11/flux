What is Flux?

Flux is a one month gathering of creative and open minded individuals in vibrant locations around the world. The main goals are facilitate connections, friendship, collaboration, explore new cultures and have fun working and living together. Also we like to promote location independent life style and cultivate technology, art and peer to peer education.

Who is Flux for?

  • Beginner and experienced Digital Nomads who is looking to connect with local and global community
  • People who interested in location independent life style but doesn't know where to start
  • Artists, hackers and technology enthusiast of any kind
